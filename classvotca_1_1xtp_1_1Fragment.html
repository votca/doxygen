Detailed Description

Rigid fragment. One conjugated segment contains several rigid fragments.

Apart from the position and orientation it has a pointer to a conjugated segment it belongs to as well as positions of atoms which belong to it of two types those which are generated by MD and those obtained by substitution of rigid copies
