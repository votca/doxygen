Detailed Description

Density-functional-based electronic coupling elements.

Evaluates DFT-based electronic coupling elements for all conjugated segments from the neighbor list. Requires molecular orbitals of two monomers and a dimer in GAUSSIAN, NWChem, or TURBOMOLE format.

B. Baumeier, J. Kirkpatrick, D. Andrienko, Phys. Chem. Chem. Phys., 12, 11103-11113, 2010
