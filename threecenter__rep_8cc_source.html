37  /*
38  * Calculate 3-center electron repulsion integrals
39  * R_{abc} = int{ phi_a(r)^DFT phi_b(r)^DFT phi_c(r')^AUX/(r-r') d3rd3r' }

45  * components actually present in shell-shell-shell combination.
