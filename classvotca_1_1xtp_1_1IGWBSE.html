Detailed Description

GWBSE-based exciton coupling elements.

Evaluates GWBSE-based exciton coupling elements for all conjugated segments from the neighbor list. Requires molecular orbitals of two monomers and a dimer in GAUSSIAN, NWChem, or TURBOMOLE (sometime) format.
